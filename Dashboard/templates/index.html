Crime Prediction for NYC Boroughs

This website shows how the boroughs of New York have been affected since 2017 in terms of unemployment and crimes. We gathered data from the official sources for our project. The data was inconsistent, missing some values and was not structured to perform operations on. After cleaning the data, we plotted it against time and other variables to get a good idea of how crime had varied over the past few decades and how unemployment rate has had an effect in different places of the New Yorkâ€™s boroughs.

Data Sources

The crime reports are our main data source, which contain attributes like crime date, time, borough, type of crime, and accurate crime latitude and longitude. Only violent crimes are included in the reports. The reports list all the violent crime committed since 2017 in New York City. With these reports we are able to locate all the crimes, know when all these crimes happened.

Business Understanding

We know that crime rate is an important index of security. However, crime rate may change greatly over time, so experience only cannot always give us the exact current status. What our project does is analyze the pervious crime reports, relate these reports with other index of the area, and finally, try to predict the crime rate. For city administrations, they may understand the trend of the variation, and make an effort to improve the security. Police stations may use that prediction and try to decide how many police force they will assign to different places. Visitors and travelers, those who are not so familiar with the city, can also benefit from our prediction and plan their trips accordingly. Therefore, in our project, we analyze the crime reports, apply various algorithms to build our model, try to find out what might be the key predictors, and then make the predictions.
